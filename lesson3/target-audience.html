Target Audience

Who:The target audience for dailybreeze.info will be audiences that are searching for a simple weather and community site. Tourists and community town members alike.

Age:18 to 100

Motivation:Searching for weather conditions and basic community information and upcoming events. Looking to get away for a weekend and experience small town, local culture.
