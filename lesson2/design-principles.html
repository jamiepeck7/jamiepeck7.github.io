White Space and Clean Design

Nike Website

The Nike webpage uses white space and clean design to display their content. They show their product, a simple shoe with a block headline describing what it is. They also use the design principle, negative space, to highlight the shoe and create a focal point. The rest of their web page follows suit with white space and a clean design.

Visual Hierarchy

Explore Idaho Website

The Explore Idaho webpage uses Visual Hierarchy on their website. The picture draws the readers attention also using the trees to create an alignment design feature. Their title is over the picture to draw the attention to the text.

Contrast

Lego Website

The Lego website uses contrast to highlight their website. The photograph uses contrast and color to draw attention to the reader. The type is white and the backgroun black, creating more contrast. Their website if full of contrast and color to draw the reader's attention and target their audience.
