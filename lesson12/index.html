Pocatello Chamber of Commerce Purpose

The Pocatello Chamber of Commerce promotes and fosters a favorable business climate through networking, advocacy, and educational opportunities for the benefit of Chamber members and our community.

What is Pocatello Chamber?

An Information Center
We are the front door to the Community, providing Community information, pamphlets and relocation information
A business Advocate
We advocate for all business members through our programs and voice on State & Local Issues
Community Development
We help enrich the quality of life through our programs including Leadership Pocatello, Beautifcation Programs and the Proud to Be Pocatllo Campaign
