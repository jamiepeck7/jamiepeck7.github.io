Join the Pocatello Chamber of Commerce Today

By joining the Pocatello Chamber, you will be part of a group of community and bussiness members that are working together to support and keep our community great!
With so many ways you can participate, the Pocatello Chamber of Commerce is a great way to give back, get involved and showcase your business to the community.
We have many committees you can join to help be a part of the community. Some particular commitees include the Local Issues Committee, the Legislative Council, the Education Committee, Chamber Champions, Chamber Chiefs and the Beautifcation Committee.
Join now to be apart of an amazing organization that will help make and keep our community great!
