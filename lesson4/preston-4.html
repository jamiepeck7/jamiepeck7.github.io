Site Name

The web site name will be Daily Breeze. The domain name chosen is dailybreeze.info. The domain name is currently avaialable on Google Domains for $12 a year.

Site Purpose

The main purpose and objective of dailybreeze.info will be for users to find out the current weather temperatures for Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven, Idaho. The site will have 6 pages. One for each town, a Storm Center page and a Gallery page. The storm center page will feature the upcoming weather conditions and what to expect. The gallery page will host pictues of the towns and activities that have been held there.

A secodary objective for dailybreeze.info will be to provide basic community information and upocoming events. It will also provide contact information for each town's Chamber of Commerce and a location map to locate the area.
