Fish Haven Days

Come enjoy Fish Haven Days this summer!

Fish Haven days will be celebrating the 100th anniversary this summer! You won't want to miss out of the extra special fun planned this year! There will be a free breakfast, followed by a ping pong drop, a local parade and our annual fish fry. Don't miss out on letting your kiddos catch our live famous trout in the fish pond. They will love running around in the water and catching a live fish with their bare hands. End the night with a magestic firework display set to music by our local Old Time Fiddlers.
